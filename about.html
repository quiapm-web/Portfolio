MY STORY

I'm Mart Angel.

A multidisciplinary Graphic Designer & Frontend Developer.

I specialize in building high-quality websites and applications. My journey started when I wrote my first line of code, and since then, I haven't stopped learning and creating.

With several years of professional experience at Aurora State College of Technology and Hi-Precision Diagnostics, I have developed a unique combination of creative and technical skills. My work includes designing branding materials, marketing visuals, motion graphics, and promotional content, as well as building and maintaining websites that showcase research, innovations, and organizational initiatives.

As a Project Technical Support II, I led video editing and graphic design efforts, created promotional campaigns, and developed a marketing website while also providing technical support for digital platforms, events, and cloud-based repositories. My earlier role as a Technical Science Research Assistant strengthened my expertise in multimedia design, visual communication, and IT support across multiple departments.

In addition to my creative work, my experience as a Jr. IT Specialist enhanced my ability to troubleshoot systems, manage networks, and ensure reliable technical operations—allowing me to approach design and development projects with both creativity and efficiency.

I am passionate about combining design, technology, and innovation to create impactful digital experiences that support business goals and user needs.
