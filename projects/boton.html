Business Context

Understanding our value proposition and market position

What We Offer

Boton provides a comprehensive range of biopesticides derived from natural sources including beneficial microorganisms, plant extracts, and minerals. Our products target specific pests and diseases while maintaining ecological balance in agricultural systems.

Who It's For

Our solutions serve commercial farmers, organic producers, greenhouse operators, and agricultural cooperatives seeking sustainable alternatives to synthetic pesticides. We also partner with agricultural extension services and government programs promoting eco-friendly farming practices.

What Sets Us Apart

Boton combines cutting-edge biotechnology with traditional knowledge to create highly effective, environmentally safe pest control solutions. Our proprietary fermentation processes ensure consistent quality and potency, backed by extensive field testing and regulatory compliance across multiple markets.

Key Stakeholders

Farmers, distributors, agricultural consultants, research institutions, regulatory bodies, environmental organizations, investors, and end consumers concerned with food safety and environmental sustainability.

Application Process

How Boton Biopesticides work in your agricultural system

1

Pest Identification & Assessment

Our specialists identify target pests and assess infestation levels to recommend appropriate biopesticide solutions.

2

Product Selection

We match the most effective biopesticide formulation based on crop type, pest characteristics, and environmental conditions.

3

Application Strategy

Customized application protocols considering timing, dosage, weather conditions, and integration with existing farm management practices.

4

Monitoring & Evaluation

Ongoing assessment of pest population reduction and crop health improvement through regular field inspections and data collection.

5

Adjustment & Optimization

Refinement of application techniques and product combinations based on monitoring results to maximize effectiveness and sustainability.
